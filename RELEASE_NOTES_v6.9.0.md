# Sol by Lycheetah 6.9.0 — THE WHITE-HOT WORLD FORGE

This is a handcrafted prototype release. It is ambitious, alive, and still
buggy; it is not presented as a finished commercial game.

## What is live

- Walkable Hearth Valley, Mystery School Cathedral and Proving Ground
- Persistent travel, discoveries, doors and world state
- Tap-to-move exploration, object approach pathing and companion following
- Live minimap, quest thread, world atlas and three camera views
- Authored Lycheetah pixel travellers, companions, keepers and challengers
- Companion body, wings, halo and pet visible throughout every world zoom
- Mystery School Commons and complete Quantum observation trial
- Named Proving Ground challengers, consent-first battles and persistent standing
- Safe-area command deck with Help and camera Reset
- Authored dialogue for the companion, shrines, House Keeper and School residents

## Honest state

Android rendering performance remains the largest known risk, especially on
large maps and distant zooms. Camera, pathing and UI have received major repairs,
but real-device edge cases remain. Four School wings beyond Quantum are visible
directional prototypes rather than complete curricula.

Built independently by Mackenzie Conor James Clark with AI collaborators under
the Lycheetah Framework.
